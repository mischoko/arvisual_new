Why getting 3D visualizations?

Cost effective

Saving money on per-image basis compared to photoshoots, quicker delivery, no rental fees, no damaged products and no logistics included.

Time-saving

No need for photoshoot management, people, space or lighting conditions + you can change the color/texture variants in a matter of minutes.

Good visuals sell

Whether you sell luxury one in a million items or mass-production furniture, your product imagery will be looking flawless and consistent.

Product validation

By creating a fictional product and marketing it to your target audience to test the demand, you can avoid the risk of losing production costs.

Quality and speed

Having 3D visualization done is infinitely faster than photoshoots + is of the same quality. You need a top quality image last-minue? No problem.

Limitless options

Do you have a crazy big idea in your head that you would never be able to execute in real life? Nothing is impossible with 3D visualizations.

Augmented Reality

Transform the shopping experience for your customers by allowing them to preview the products in the comfort of their homes. Empower your sales representatives to showcase the entire product line with just a few swipes of smart device.

3D modelling & packshots

Having 3D models of your products is an absolute must nowadays, whether you are cooperating with architects, designers or you simply wish to cut your expenses on the photoshoots by using 3D visualizations.

Getting appropriate, optimized and high-quality 3D models is crucial in order to achieve the desired result. Based on your goals and needs, our 3D team will be able to guide you through all the technical aspects, such as polygon count, texture quality or which 3D format to use for what purpose.

Packshots are basically an exact 3D representation of your products suitable for both web and print use in catalogues or brochures. The main advantage is that you don't need to have all the product variations produced physically, in fact you don't even need to have the product in stock at all - all we need is a visual reference and a couple of measrement details.

Also, the imagery can be entirely customized - white, grey, colorful or transparent background, soft or harsh light, shadows or no shadows at all, any camera angle and focal length you can imagine and lots more...
